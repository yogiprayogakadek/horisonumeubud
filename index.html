Horison Ume Suites and Villas Ubud

Nestled among the lush valleys of Ubud and surrounded by the enchanting forest and idyllic river, Horison Ume Suites and Villas Ubud is a luxury haven of tranquility, enhanced with five-star amenities and facilities, as well as impeccable service. Situated in Kelabang Moding, a traditional Balinese village just 15 minutes away from the central Ubud, the eco-friendly hotel provides ideal stunning wedding and event venues in ubud, suites boasting breathtaking valley views and opulent honeymoon villas with spacious living areas.
